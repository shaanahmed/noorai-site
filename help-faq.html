Help & FAQ

Most support requests are answered below. Please check this page before contacting support.

How do I change to Assamese?

Open the app, go to Settings, then choose Language and select Assamese (অসমীয়া). If some text does not refresh immediately, reopen the app once.

Why is the audio not playing?

Quran audio may require an active internet connection because recitations are streamed. First check your internet connection, then try stopping playback and starting again. If the problem continues, switch to another reciter and retry.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Open Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Google account used for the original purchase and that your internet connection is active.

Why is Learn AI still locked?

If purchases are not configured in a development build, premium access behavior may differ. In production, premium access depends on purchase status and successful entitlement sync through the billing infrastructure.

Does NoorAI work offline?

Core Quran reading and stored local content are designed to be available offline. Some features, such as audio playback and certain third-party integrations, may require internet access.

How do I contact support?

Email noorai.support@gmail.com with the subject line NoorAI Support Request.

If your issue is not covered above, contact support directly.
