NoorAI: Quran & Islamic Guide

Quran, recitation, Namaz, Duas, Qibla, and Islamic guidance in one app.

NoorAI: Quran & Islamic Guide is an all-in-one Islamic app for Muslims who want to read Quran, listen to recitation, learn Namaz, make daily Duas, find Qibla, and stay connected to faith with practical Islamic tools.

Whether you are looking for a Quran app, Islamic learning app, Dua app, Namaz learning app, or Muslim daily companion, NoorAI brings everything together in one clean experience.

  • Quran reading in a focused interface with multilingual verse support
  • Quran audio recitation for reflection, memorization, and daily routine
  • Duas for daily life including health, family, rizq, protection, travel, stress, and gratitude
  • Step-by-step Namaz guidance for learners and daily refresher use
  • Islamic daily tools including Qibla direction and calendar-based reminders
  • Junab AI guidance with clearer chat controls and improved reliability
