Privacy Policy

Effective date: 01 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NoorAI: Quran & Islamic Guide, published by NoorAI Studio, handles information when you use the app and related support pages.

Overview

NoorAI is designed to minimize data collection wherever possible. Core Quran reading content is available primarily through local app resources, while certain features may rely on third-party services or network access.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store limited local information on your device to support app functionality, including:

  • selected language
  • appearance or theme preference
  • reading progress
  • completed surahs
  • local streak or usage indicators
  • app preferences needed for normal functionality

This information is generally stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to NoorAI Studio servers by default.

Audio And Network Features

Some app features, including Quran audio playback, may require network access and may communicate with third-party services in order to retrieve audio or related content.

In-App Purchases

If premium or paid features are enabled, purchase handling may involve Google Play Billing and RevenueCat or similar purchase infrastructure. Purchase-related information is processed by the relevant platform or service provider according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Advertising

If advertising is enabled in production builds, the app may use Google AdMob or similar ad services. These services may collect device identifiers, usage signals, and other data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party services, platforms, or links, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play
  • Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat
  • external web links
  • audio content providers

NoorAI Studio does not control the privacy practices of third-party providers. Users should review the privacy policies of those providers separately.

AI And Informational Features

AI-related explanations in the app are intended for informational and educational assistance. They are not a substitute for qualified scholarly advice, legal advice, or religious rulings.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed toward children under the minimum age required by applicable digital consent laws in the user’s jurisdiction.

Data Security

We aim to use standard platform and network protections where applicable. However, no software, device, or networked service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

International Use

Users are responsible for complying with the laws and regulations applicable in their own region or country when using the app.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date when appropriate.
