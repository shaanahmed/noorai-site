Privacy Policy

Effective date: 03 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NoorAI: Quran & Islamic Guide, published by NoorAI Studio, handles information when you use the app and related support pages.

Overview

NoorAI is designed to minimize data collection wherever possible. Core Quran reading content is available primarily through local app resources. NoorAI does not require user-created app accounts for core usage.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store limited local information on your device to support app functionality, including:

  • selected language
  • theme preference
  • reading progress
  • usage counters
  • favorites
  • offline AI history
  • cached calendar and location preferences
  • other app preferences required for normal functionality

This information is generally stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to NoorAI Studio servers by default.

Optional Location Permission

If you enable location features, NoorAI may use approximate or precise location data to calculate more exact local prayer times and support location-aware Islamic calendar or country selection features.

Location access is optional and can be changed at any time through your Android app permission settings.

Optional Microphone Permission

If you enable microphone access, NoorAI may use it for optional voice search or voice-driven premium features. Microphone permission is optional and can be disabled in Android app settings.

Audio And Network Features

Some app features, including Quran audio playback, may require network access and may communicate with external recitation endpoints or third-party services to retrieve audio or related content.

Advertising And Purchases

If premium or paid features are enabled, purchase handling may involve Google Play Billing and RevenueCat or similar purchase infrastructure. Purchase-related information is processed by those providers according to their terms and privacy policies.

If advertising is enabled in production builds, the app may use Google AdMob or similar ad services. These services may collect device identifiers, usage signals, and other data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Data Sharing And Third-Party Services

NoorAI does not provide social sharing features where user location is published to other users. Some app capabilities still rely on third-party services and platforms, including:

  • Google Play
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat
  • external web links
  • audio content providers

NoorAI Studio does not control third-party privacy practices. Please review those providers directly.

AI And Informational Features

AI-related explanations in the app are intended for informational and educational assistance. They are not a substitute for qualified scholarly advice, legal advice, or religious rulings.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed toward children under the minimum age required by applicable digital consent laws in the user’s jurisdiction.

Data Security

We aim to use standard platform and network protections where applicable. However, no software, device, or networked service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

International Use

Users are responsible for complying with the laws and regulations applicable in their own region or country when using the app.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date when appropriate.
