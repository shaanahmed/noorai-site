1. Introduction

Welcome to NoorAI: Quran & Islamic Guide ("NoorAI", "the App", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using NoorAI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. App Features Overview

Core Islamic Features

  • Full Quran reading with Arabic text, tashkeel (diacritics), and multilingual translation
  • Quran audio recitation with text-to-speech playback
  • Daily Duas (supplications) for life situations
  • 99 Names of Allah with Arabic, transliteration, and meaning
  • Namaz (Salah) step-by-step learning guide
  • Qibla direction finder using device location and compass
  • Islamic calendar and daily reminders

Junab AI — Islamic AI Assistant

  • Conversational Islamic guidance powered by AI
  • Dua recommendations based on user context
  • Quran verse and Hadith explanations
  • Personalized Islamic Q&A
  • Chat history management (clear chat feature)

Learning & Tools

  • Arabic learning activities
  • Pronunciation audio playback
  • Multilingual support (17 languages)
  • Premium subscription features
  • Promo code system for creators

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Junab AI Chat Input: When you use Junab AI, the text or voice messages you type or speak are processed to generate responses. We do not permanently store your individual chat conversations on our servers. Conversations may be cleared by you at any time.
  • Speech Input: If you use voice input for Junab AI, audio is converted to text on-device or via the device's speech recognition service. We do not record or store raw audio.
  • Promo Codes: If you enter a promotional code, it is verified against our database without storing personally identifiable information.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostics and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: General feature usage patterns (which screens are visited, frequency of use) collected in anonymized, aggregated form.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymized error and crash data to improve app stability.

3.3 Location Information

The app requests access to your device location solely to calculate the Qibla direction. Location data is used in real time and is not stored, transmitted to our servers, or shared with third parties.

Location permission is optional. You may deny it and the Qibla feature will be unavailable.

3.4 Purchase Information

Payment transactions are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We may receive confirmation of purchase status (active, expired, cancelled) through RevenueCat and Google Play Billing to verify your subscription.

3.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number unless you contact us directly.
  • We do not collect biometric data.
  • We do not build personal profiles or sell your data to advertisers.

4. Junab AI — Specific Data Practices

Junab AI is our built-in Islamic artificial intelligence assistant. Because AI features involve processing user input, we want to be fully transparent.

How Junab AI works:

  • Your messages are sent to an AI processing service to generate Islamic guidance responses.
  • Messages are processed in real time to produce answers.
  • NoorAI does not retain your Junab AI conversation history on our servers beyond the active session.
  • You can clear your local chat history at any time from within the app.

What Junab AI does NOT do:

  • It does not store personally identifiable conversations linked to your identity.
  • It does not use your conversations to train AI models without your explicit consent.
  • It does not share your queries with advertisers or third-party marketing services.
  • It does not access your contacts, photos, files, or other apps.

Voice Input for Junab AI:

Voice input uses your device's built-in speech recognition (e.g., Google Speech Services on Android). This is subject to Google's own privacy policy. NoorAI does not receive or store the raw audio recording.

  • App settings and preferences (such as tashkeel on/off, language selection) are stored locally on your device using secure device storage.
  • Subscription status and promo code validation data stored in Firebase Firestore are protected using Firebase security rules and Google Cloud security standards.

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we process.
  • No system is 100% secure. If you have concerns about data security, please contact us at noorai.support@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy
